Sample behavioral waveforms for design file "FIFO_BUFF.v"

The following waveforms show the behavior of scfifo megafunction for the chosen set of parameters in design "FIFO_BUFF.v". The design "FIFO_BUFF.v" has a depth of 8192 words of 36 bits each. The fifo is in legacy synchronous mode. The data becomes available after 'rdreq' is asserted; 'rdreq' acts as a read request.
